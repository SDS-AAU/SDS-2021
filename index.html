SOCIAL DATA SCIENCE SEMESTER (SDS)

and Management at Aalborg University offers a new option for the 3rd semester of their master's programmes, the Social Data Science specialization semester (SDS). Immerse yourself into the increasingly relevant and fast-emerging sector of data analytics, starting September 2018. Through this programme, you will learn cutting-edge, requisite skills in, practical terms, and be able to apply them to real-world issues in various fields. Data continues to grow exponentially. From Social media posts, sensor signals on smartphones, smart home devices to the increasing digitisation of companies and public organisations, data has seen unprecedented granularity.
